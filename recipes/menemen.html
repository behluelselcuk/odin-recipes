Menemen

Description

Menemen is a classic Turkish breakfast dish made with scrambled eggs, tomatoes, green peppers, and spices, often enhanced with onions and feta cheese

It is typically served hot with crusty bread, making for a hearty, flavorful, and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Chopped onion
  • Diced red bell pepper
  • Diced Aleppo chiles
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped tomato
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Large eggs, beaten
  • Thinly sliced basil leaves
  • Crumbled feta cheese

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, chiles, and red pepper flakes. Cook and stir until onions are soft and translucent, 5 to 6 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, with their juices, and season with salt and pepper. Cook until tomatoes are softened, 4 to 6 minutes.
  5. Add eggs to the skillet, tilting the skillet until eggs cover skillet completely. Do not mix. Cook until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Top with basil and feta cheese.
