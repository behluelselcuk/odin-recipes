Iskender Kebab

Description

Iskender Kebab is a traditional Turkish dish consisting of thinly sliced, seasoned lamb or beef served over a bed of pita bread, topped with a rich tomato sauce and melted butter.

It is often accompanied by a side of yogurt and garnished with grilled vegetables, creating a flavorful combination of savory and tangy tastes.

Ingredients

  • Pita bread rounds
  • Olive oil
  • Doner meat
  • Chopped onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Ground cumin
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Melted Butter
  • Greek yogurt
  • Chopped fresh parsley
